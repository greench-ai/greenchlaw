# Luxembourg Cannabis Regulations ⚖️
*For SativaBox.lu and EU compliance*

## Luxembourg Cannabis Law (2026)

### Hemp / Industrial Hemp
- Hemp with ≤0.3% THC: **Legal** for cultivation, sale, and products
- EU-approved seed list must be used
- Luxembourg follows EU Novel Food Regulation for CBD products

### CBD Products
- CBD products with ≤0.3% THC: **Legal** in Luxembourg
- Novel Food regulation: CBD as novel food requires EFSA approval
- Products already on market before 1997: grandfathered
- Health claims on products: **Prohibited**
- "Cures," "treats," "prevents" disease: **Illegal claims**

### THC Products
- Cannabis with >0.3% THC: **Illegal** for recreational use
- Medical cannabis: Available by prescription only
- Personal cultivation: **Illegal**
- Sale/purchase: **Illegal** outside licensed scheme

### What SativaBox Can Sell ✅
- CBD flowers (≤0.3% THC)
- CBD oils, edibles, topicals (≤0.3% THC)
- Hemp seeds (non-germinating)
- Grow equipment
- Smoking accessories
- Clothing and merchandise

### Prohibited ❌
- THC flowers or products (>0.3% THC)
- Vaping products with vitamin E acetate
- Products making health claims
- Germinating seeds
- Anything facilitating illegal cultivation

## EU Cross-Border Rules
- Each EU country has different CBD/THC thresholds
- Germany: ≤0.2% THC
- France: ≤0.3% THC
- Luxembourg: ≤0.3% THC
- Check destination country before shipping

## Packaging Requirements
- THC% clearly labeled
- Net weight
- Producer information
- Batch number
- CBD/THC disclaimer
- Keep out of reach of children
