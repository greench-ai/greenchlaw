# Indoor Grow Guide 🌱
*Source: SativaBox.lu + industry best practices*

## Equipment Checklist (SativaBox Products)

### Lighting
- **LED (recommended):** Luminaria Fission LED 300W V2.0 — full spectrum, low heat, 300W
- **HPS:** 250-600W for flower
- **PPFD target:** 400-800 µmol/m²/s for veg, 800-1500 for flower

### Space
- **Tent:** 60x60x160cm (small), 100x100x200cm (medium), 120x120x200cm (large)
- **SativaBox:** Pure Square Black Pot (0.5L-25L), fabric pots for air pruning

### Ventilation
- **Inline fan:** Match to tent size (6" for large tents, 4" for medium)
- **Clip fan:** Air circulation inside tent
- **Odor control:** Carbon filter — essential for smell management

### Nutrients
- **Veg stage:** High N, lower P/K (NPK ~3-1-2)
- **Flower stage:** Lower N, higher P/K (NPK ~1-3-3)
- **Brands available at SativaBox:** BioBizz, Plagron, Canna

## Growth Stages

### Seedling (Week 1-2)
- 18-24h light
- Low nutrients (1/4 strength)
- High humidity (70-80%)
- Soil or root cube

### Vegetative (Week 2-8)
- 18/6 light cycle
- Full nutrients from week 3
- LST (low-stress training) to shape plant
- Topping after 4-6 nodes for bushier plants

### Flowering (Week 8-12)
- 12/12 light cycle
- Lower nitrogen, higher PK
- Watch for hermies (light leaks)
- Flush 1-2 weeks before harvest

### Harvest
- **Trichome check:** 80%+ milky white, some amber
- **Timing:** Morning harvest, after lights off 2-3 hours
- **Dry:** 15-20°C, 45-55% humidity, 7-14 days
- **Cure:** Airtight jars, burp daily, 2+ weeks minimum

## Common Problems

| Problem | Cause | Solution |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Yellow leaves (bottom) | N deficiency | Add nitrogen |
| Yellow leaves (top) | pH lockout | Check pH (6.0-7.0 soil) |
| Brown tips | Nutrient burn | Flush, reduce nutrients |
| Stretching | Too little light | Move lights closer |
| White powder | Powdery mildew | Increase airflow, neem oil |
| Spider mites | Dry, stagnant air | Increase RH, predator mites |
| Hermaphrodites | Light stress | Check for light leaks |
| Foxtailing | Heat stress / genetics | Lower temperature (<28°C) |
