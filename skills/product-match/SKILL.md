# 🛒 Product Match Skill

## Purpose
Match customer needs to SativaBox products based on symptoms, preferences, and use case.

## Knowledge Base
- SativaBox product catalog (https://sativabox.lu)
- Product categories: GROW, SMOKE, CBD & HEMP, SEEDS

## Input Mapping

| Customer Need | Product Category |
|---------------|----------------|
| Better sleep | CBD & HEMP → CBNight Candies, CBD oils |
| Anxiety relief | CBD & HEMP → CBday/CBImmuno, CBD flowers |
| Pain / inflammation | CBD & HEMP → CBImmuno, topical CBD |
| Relaxation | CBD & HEMP → Full spectrum CBD oil |
| Growing setup | GROW → LED lights, pots, nutrients |
| Smoking accessories | SMOKE → Bongs, pipes, papers, grinders |
| Beginner growing | SEEDS → Autoflower seeds |
| Best yields | GROW → LED 300W+, large tent, SCRoG |

## Process
1. Understand customer use case
2. Map to product category
3. Recommend specific products with price
4. Explain why product fits their need
5. Mention alternatives for different budgets
